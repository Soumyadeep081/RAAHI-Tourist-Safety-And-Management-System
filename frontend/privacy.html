Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Raahi, trust is our core product. We only collect the data necessary to keep you safe.

1. Location Data

Your real-time location is stored securely and is only shared with your designated emergency contacts, or when you explicitly trigger an SOS alert.

2. Data Retention

Location history is retained for 30 days for your safety, after which it is permanently deleted unless required for an active emergency investigation.
